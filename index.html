CANA Frequently Asked Questions

1. What is CANA?

CANA ($CANA) is a cultural movement that bridges blockchain technology with real-world products. It combines physical items (like streetwear and collectibles) with NFTs to create verifiable, one-of-a-kind ownership experiences.

2. How do RWA-Backed NFTs work?

Each CANA NFT represents a physical product. When you mint or buy one, you receive both a digital NFT in your wallet and the corresponding physical item with a unique QR code linking them on-chain.

3. What products can I own as RWA-Backed NFTs?

CANA offers streetwear, apparel, lifestyle gear, and limited-edition collectibles. Each product is tied to an NFT that proves authenticity and ownership history.

4. What is the "One-of-One Protocol"?

Before reselling a CANA NFT, you must destroy the physical product and submit video proof. This ensures only one physical item exists per NFT at any time, maintaining rarity.

5. How does the Destruction Protocol work?

1. Record a video clearly showing the product's destruction (with QR code visible)
2. Submit it via CANA Marketplace
3. Wait for verification before reselling the NFT

6. What happens when I resell my CANA NFT?

You receive 50% of the resale value. The other 50% funds a brand-new physical product for the buyer, tied to the same NFT. The original item is destroyed to preserve uniqueness.

7. How does the QR code system work?

Each physical product has a unique QR code that:
- Links to its NFT on the blockchain
- Shows ownership history
- Prevents counterfeits
- Enables resale with automatic royalties

8. Can I use CANA products in digital worlds?

Yes! CANA is developing integrations with gaming/metaverse platforms to let you equip avatars with your NFT-linked physical items (like hoodies or sneakers).

9. What benefits do NFT holders get?

- Early access to new drops
- Time-based perks for long-term holders
- In-game boosts (when integrated)
- 50% resale royalties
- Exclusive events/airdrops

10. Where can I buy/sell CANA NFTs?

The official CANA Marketplace will handle all transactions, including:
- Verified resales
- Destruction verification
- Automatic royalty splits (50/50 between seller and CANA)

11. What blockchain does CANA use?

Currently on TON (as a meme coin), with plans for:
- A dedicated Layer-2 chain on TON for NFTs
- Multi-chain deployment (Solana, Ethereum, Polygon, etc.)

12. How is CANA different from other NFT projects?

CANA uniquely ties NFTs to physical items with enforced scarcity (via destruction), real utility (wearable products), and circular economics (50% resale royalties).

13. What happens if my physical product is damaged?

If the QR code is intact, it remains verifiable. If destroyed, you can initiate the resale process (with destruction proof) or contact support for edge cases.

14. Can I transfer my NFT without destroying the item?

No. The physical product must be destroyed before transferring the NFT to maintain the one-of-one guarantee. This is enforced by smart contracts.

15. How does CANA prevent counterfeit QR codes?

Each QR code is:
- Cryptographically tied to the NFT
- Recorded on-chain at minting
- Invalidated after destruction
- Scannable to show full history

16. What's CANA's long-term vision?

To create a circular economy where:
- Every physical product has digital twin
- Ownership is provable and valuable
- Resales fund new creations sustainably
- Culture is co-owned by the community

17. Will there be a DAO?

Yes! Future plans include decentralized governance where NFT holders can vote on:
- Product designs
- Partnerships
- Treasury allocations

18. How do I verify authenticity before buying?

Always:
1. Scan the QR code to check blockchain records
2. Confirm the NFT matches the physical item
3. Buy only through official CANA channels

19. What if I don't want to destroy my item?

You can keep it forever! The Destruction Protocol only applies if you choose to resell the NFT. Physical ownership doesn't expire.
